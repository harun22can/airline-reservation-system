Flight Rules and Regulations

1. Baggage Rules

  • Carry-on baggage must be maximum 8 kg and 55x40x23 cm in size.
  • You are entitled to 1 carry-on bag and 1 personal item (bag, laptop bag) for cabin baggage.
  • Check-in baggage has a maximum weight limit of 23 kg.
  • Dangerous materials (flammable, explosive, sharp objects) cannot be carried in baggage.

2. Check-in Rules

  • Online check-in starts 24 hours before the flight and ends 2 hours before.
  • You must arrive at the airport at least 2 hours in advance.
  • For international flights, it is recommended to arrive at least 3 hours in advance.
  • Have your valid ID or passport ready during check-in.

3. Security Rules

  • Remove all electronic devices when passing through security check.
  • Liquid substances cannot exceed 100 ml and must be carried in a transparent bag.
  • Sharp, piercing objects and liquids cannot be carried in carry-on baggage.
  • Follow the instructions of security personnel.

4. In-Flight Rules

  • Mobile phones must be set to airplane mode during the flight.
  • When the seatbelt sign is on, fasten your seatbelt and remain seated.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited.
  • Alcohol consumption is limited and under the control of the crew.

5. Cancellation and Change Rules

  • Ticket cancellation must be done 24 hours before the flight.
  • Refund amounts in cancellation transactions may vary according to ticket type.
  • Additional fees may be charged for ticket changes.
  • In case of no-show, the ticket fee is not refunded.

6. Health and Special Conditions

  • Pregnant passengers must bring a doctor's report after the 28th week.
  • Special service requests for disabled passengers must be notified in advance.
  • A medical report may be required for sick passengers.
  • Special dietary needs must be informed in advance.
